# Skincare Ingredient Glossary

{{<figure src="/images/TCMHerbalLab_800.png" width="400" >}}

Here are the ingredients grouped by the specific skin concerns they are most effective at treating.

## 1. Anti-Aging & Fine Lines

These ingredients focus on boosting collagen, increasing cell turnover, and firming the skin.

- **Retinol (Vitamin A):**  
The gold standard for reducing wrinkles and resurfacing skin.

- **Adenosine:**  
Energizes skin cells to smooth out the skin surface.

- **White Ginseng (Powder or Extract):**  
Revitalizes circulation and improves skin elasticity.

- **Pitera (Galactomyces Ferment Filtrate):**  
Enhances cell turnover for a more youthful, refined texture.

- **Solomon's Seal (Polygonatum):**  
Traditionally used to provide a "lifting" and firming effect.

## 2. Hydration & Moisture Barrier Repair

Look for these if your skin feels tight, flaky, or sensitive due to a damaged protective layer.

- **Ceramide:**  
Essential for rebuilding the skin's natural "glue" to prevent moisture loss.

- **Hyaluronic Acid:**  
Acts like a sponge to pull water into the skin.

- **Squalane:**  
Mimics natural skin oils to soften and lock in moisture without greasiness.

- **Birch Sap (Betula Alba):**  
Provides deep, mineral-rich hydration for sensitive skin.

- **Snail Mucin:**  
Repairs the barrier while providing deep, long-lasting hydration.

## 3. Brightening & Hyperpigmentation

These ingredients help fade dark spots, acne scars, and overall dullness.

- **Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid):**  
Brightens the complexion and protects against future sun damage.

- **Niacinamide (Vitamin B3):**  
Fades dark spots and evens out skin tone.

- **Rice Water:**  
A gentle traditional remedy for a "glass skin" glow.

- **White Lily:**  
Known for its ability to inhibit melanin and brighten the skin.

- **Green Plum (Maesil):**  
Uses natural acids to gently exfoliate dead, dull skin cells.

## 4. Acne & Oil Control

Use these to treat active breakouts, unclog pores, and balance sebum production.

- **Propolis Extract:**  
Naturally antibacterial; heals pimples and prevents new ones.
