The 4 Types of Dampness Affecting Your Skin: A TCM Guide

What Is “Dampness” in TCM? In Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), dampness (湿 shī) describes a pathological state where excess moisture accumulates in the body. Think of it like humidity you can’t escape — heavy, sticky, and lingering. Unlike acute inflammation, dampness is chronic and persistent. It shows up as: Skin that never feels fully clean Puffiness that won’t go away Acne that heals slowly and leaves marks A dull, grayish complexion The good news?
